"Assignment overview"

The purpose of the assignment was to teach students about PAXOS. In regard to PAXOS, it was
intended to teach the student about consensus. The student will learn how PAXOS can handle failures, and the types
of failures PAXOS can handle. Additionally, the student will
learn more about systems with more complex RMI handling's.

“Technical impression”s

Overall, I really enjoyed this project. I was initially pretty intimidated by this project.
PAXOS, honestly, did not make sense at first. It was a bit confusing about who should take on what roles, or even reasoning
about how consensus is reached in the protocol. Technically speaking, the use of RMI was much easier this time around, or even just thinking about
network communication in a system like. A issue that I ran into was: "If all nodes act as all roles why do i have to accept AND learn". However, if a node fails to receive
and accept it would not commit. Therefore, it realise on its peers to inform it on new values.
There is plenty more that can be achieved with this project. I want to make the leader/leaner programmatically fail. Right now leaders can fail,
as shown my testBash. However, it could be interesting for a leader to go down come back up.
